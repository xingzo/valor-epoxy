# SEO Strategy - Valor Epoxy

Date: 2026-03-02  
Domain: `www.valorepoxy.com`  
Business Type: Local service (epoxy flooring, concrete coatings, polishing)  
Primary Market: Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex

## 1) Discovery Summary

- **Business goals:** Increase qualified quote requests for residential and commercial coating projects across DFW.
- **Target audiences:** Homeowners (garage, patio, pool deck), property managers, light commercial and industrial operators.
- **Current site status:** Small footprint (6 crawlable pages), low topical depth, weak location coverage, limited schema depth beyond homepage.
- **Constraints (assumed):** Lean production capacity, moderate marketing budget, need for measurable gains inside 12 months.
- **Core KPIs:** Organic sessions, local keyword coverage, quote form leads, call clicks, map pack visibility, CWV health.

## 2) Strategic Direction

- Build a **service x location** SEO engine (high-intent pages for each core service in each priority city).
- Expand from a brochure site into a **local authority resource** with proof content (projects, FAQs, process transparency).
- Strengthen E-E-A-T through credentials, project-level evidence, and trust/response systems.
- Improve technical hygiene (schema, internal links, indexation control, performance targets) to support faster ranking velocity.

## 3) KPI Targets


| Metric           | Baseline                          | 3 Month                      | 6 Month           | 12 Month          |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ----------------- | ----------------- |
| Organic Traffic  | Low (assume 300-600/mo)           | +35%                         | +90%              | +180%             |

## 4) Priority Keyword Themes

- **Commercial intent:** epoxy flooring dallas, concrete coatings fort worth, garage floor coating plano, metallic epoxy frisco.
- **Service intent:** polyaspartic floor coating, concrete polishing service, flake system flooring, industrial floor coatings.
- **Problem intent:** cracked garage floor fix, oil-stain resistant garage flooring, slip-resistant patio coating.
- **Trust intent:** epoxy flooring cost dallas, epoxy vs polyaspartic texas, best epoxy contractor dfw.

## 5) E-E-A-T and Conversion Trust Plan

- Add team/operator credibility: years in business, certifications, insurance, service guarantees.
- Publish first-hand project case studies with before/after photos, surface condition, process, timeline, and outcome.
- Standardize review capture and public response cadence (Google profile + on-site testimonials).
- Add explicit service-area proof blocks (recent jobs by city + map references).

## 6) Technical Foundation Targets

- **Schema rollout:** `LocalBusiness`, `Service`, `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`, `Review`, `ImageObject`, `ContactPoint`.
- **Mobile-first CWV targets:** LCP <2.5s, INP <200ms, CLS <0.1 on top 20 landing pages.
- **Indexing:** Canonical consistency, XML sitemap split by page type, no orphan service/location pages.
- **AI search readiness:** concise answer blocks, entity clarity, FAQ sections, machine-readable business and service data.

## 7) Resource Model

- **Owner/PM:** prioritization, approvals, quality control.
- **SEO lead (part-time):** keyword mapping, on-page standards, technical QA.
- **Content producer:** 2-4 pages/week (service/location/case/FAQ mix).
- **Developer support:** schema, templates, internal linking automation, CWV fixes.

## 8) Risks and Mitigation

- **Risk:** Thin location pages -> **Mitigation:** require unique local proof + FAQs + city-specific project examples.
- **Risk:** Inconsistent lead quality -> **Mitigation:** intent-specific CTAs and stronger qualification fields.
- **Risk:** Competitor content velocity -> **Mitigation:** publish in topical clusters with internal link reinforcement.
- **Risk:** Slow execution -> **Mitigation:** template-first publishing and monthly sprint checkpoints.

## 9) 12-Month Success Criteria

- 100+ indexed, quality-controlled pages across services, locations, and resources.
- Stable map pack visibility in top-priority DFW cities for 8-12 money keywords.
- Organic-sourced quote pipeline with measurable month-over-month growth.
- Technical SEO and CWV compliance maintained as content scales.
