# Implementation Roadmap - Valor Epoxy

Date: 2026-03-02  
Scope: 12-month SEO execution plan in 4 phases

## Phase 1 - Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

### Objectives
- Establish technical baseline and publish core conversion assets.
- Build initial service architecture and analytics instrumentation.

### Deliverables
- Technical setup: sitemap segmentation, canonical checks, basic schema framework.
- Core page updates: home, about, services index, contact, quote.
- New money pages: 3 priority services.
- Tracking setup: GA4 events, GSC monitoring, call/quote attribution.

### Dependencies
- Access to analytics and search console.
- Approved page templates and copy framework.

### Success Metrics
- All core pages indexed and technically valid.
- First service pages live with complete on-page elements.
- Lead tracking events verified end-to-end.

## Phase 2 - Expansion (Weeks 5-12)

### Objectives
- Expand local landing footprint and create proof content loops.
- Capture city and long-tail intent in DFW priority zones.

### Deliverables
- 8-12 location/service pages.
- 4-6 case studies with before/after media.
- Resource hub launch (minimum 6 articles).
- Internal linking rollout across service/location/proof pages.

### Dependencies
- Consistent project photography and job documentation.
- Editorial production cadence (weekly).

### Success Metrics
- 25+ indexed pages.
- 30+ non-brand keywords in Top 20.
- Measurable growth in organic quote starts.

## Phase 3 - Scale (Weeks 13-24)

### Objectives
- Build topical authority clusters and strengthen off-site trust signals.
- Improve UX and speed on high-intent landing pages.

### Deliverables
- Additional 25-40 pages (city expansions, advanced service pages, comparison guides).
- Link earning and local citation reinforcement.
- CWV optimization sprint for top landing pages.
- FAQ/schema expansion across key templates.

### Dependencies
- Dev support for performance and structured data improvements.
- Outreach process for mentions and links.

### Success Metrics
- 60+ indexed pages.
- 75+ non-brand terms in Top 20.
- 20-30% increase in conversion rate from organic landings.

## Phase 4 - Authority (Months 7-12)

### Objectives
- Defend and grow rankings with authority assets, PR, and continuous optimization.
- Scale trust and conversion efficiency.

### Deliverables
- Thought leadership assets (original local insights/research).
- Digital PR campaigns and strategic partnerships.
- Quarterly page refresh and CRO testing cycles.
- Advanced schema implementation and entity reinforcement.

### Dependencies
- Ongoing content operations and reporting discipline.
- Access to customer success stories and review workflows.

### Success Metrics
- 120+ indexed pages with quality control.
- Strong first-page presence for priority DFW terms.
- Stable month-over-month growth in qualified organic leads.

## Resource and Ownership Matrix

- **Owner/Operations:** approval, offer strategy, local proof sourcing.
- **SEO Lead:** roadmap ownership, keyword mapping, monthly performance reviews.
- **Writer/Editor:** content production and refresh cycles.
- **Developer:** template updates, schema, speed, technical QA.
- **Optional outreach support:** citations, partnerships, digital PR.

## Risk Register

- **Execution slowdown:** use template libraries and sprint planning.
- **Quality dilution at scale:** enforce page quality gates and editorial QA.
- **Attribution gaps:** verify analytics and call tracking monthly.
- **Ranking volatility:** diversify across service, location, and informational intent.

## Reporting Cadence

- Weekly: production and indexing status.
- Monthly: rankings, traffic, leads, and conversion rate movement.
- Quarterly: strategy recalibration, content refresh priorities, and technical debt plan.
